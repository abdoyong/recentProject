I

Project Title

Vehicle Speed and Trajectory Analysis using Computer Vision

A comprehensive system to analyze traffic patterns through advanced computer vision techniques.

II

Problem Statement

Accurate measurement of vehicle speeds and analysis of lane usage patterns are crucial for traffic management, road safety assessment, and transportation planning. Traditional methods using radar guns or loop sensors are costly, require physical installation, and offer limited coverage.

üí∞ High Cost of Traditional Methods
üöß Need for Physical Installation
üìç Limited Coverage Area
III

Proposed Solution & Methodology

Our solution leverages computer vision techniques to analyze traffic video footage and extract vehicle speed and lane usage information.

1

Object Detection

Using pre-trained models (YOLO) to identify vehicles in each frame.

2

Multi-Object Tracking (MOT)

Tracking individual vehicles across different frames.

3

Speed Estimation

Conversion from pixel coordinates to real-world coordinates and temporal analysis.

4

Lane Detection & Identification

Perspective transformation and geometric analysis.
